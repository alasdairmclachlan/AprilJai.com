APRIL JAI

apriljai.com

April Jai is London's new up and coming country artist. After studying music in Edinburgh, she moved to London to become a household name. Blending her love of pop and country, she has developed a voice of her own, drawing influence from artists such as Taylor Swift, Kacey Musgraves and The Civil Wars. Her debut EP "The Last Goodbye" reached #2 on the iTunes UK Country Chart. "The combination of great songwriting and April's voice makes for a record that will be played again and again." - Belles & Gals “Each song gives April’s voice a chance to shine in a different way, and that is a sign of a very talented artist, and someone who is going to go far in the music industry.” -Fierce & Fabulous Revolution
